6  Character Races

Demography

The allowed PC character races in Old School D&D are Human, Dwarf, Elf, Half-Elf, Halfling, Half-Orc, Gnome and Goblin. 50% of the humanoid population is human, 10% are dwarves, 10% are halflings, 10% are elves, 5 are half-orcs, and 5% are goblins and gnomes.

Dwarves

Description

Brave, stalwart folk as sturdy as the stone kingdoms they carve inside mountains.

You know the Common and Dwarvish languages.

Stout

Start with +2HP. Roll HP per level with advantage
